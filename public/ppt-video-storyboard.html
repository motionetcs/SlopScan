3-Minute Video Script

Opening: Hi, this is SlopScan, a review trust analyzer for online shopping. The problem is that star ratings are easy to trust but hard to verify. Reviews can be repetitive, promotional, vague, or even unrelated to the product.

Demo flow: I start by pasting a product URL. SlopScan detects the marketplace and moves me into the review analysis flow. Since marketplaces often restrict direct review access, the app asks me to paste or import the reviews I want checked.

Dashboard: The dashboard shows an overall trust score, review-risk score, human-like review percentage, suspicious review share, and confidence level. The Buyer Issues tab extracts repeated grounded problems. The Review X-Ray tab shows suspicious reviews and explains the signals. The Claims + Q&A tab compares seller claims with buyer evidence.

Closing: SlopScan does not claim to prove fake reviews. It flags suspicious patterns and explains the evidence, helping buyers make better decisions from the reviews they already see.

Short Pitch

SlopScan is a review trust layer for online shopping. Users paste a product URL, paste or import reviews, and get an explainable trust dashboard. It flags suspicious review patterns, repeated phrases, generic praise, product mismatch, unsupported seller claims, and grounded buyer issues. It helps shoppers understand the review quality behind a rating before they buy.
